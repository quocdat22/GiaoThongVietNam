Yield Sign

Warning

Description

The yield sign indicates that drivers must slow down and yield the right of way to traffic on the through road. You should stop if necessary and proceed only when it's safe to do so without interfering with through traffic.

When You'll See It

  • At intersections where you need to give way to traffic on the main road
  • At the end of acceleration lanes on highways
  • At roundabout entrances
